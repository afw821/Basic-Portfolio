About Me

My name is Alex Watkins. I'm married and live with my wife, Crystal Watkins, in Atlanta, Ga. I grew up close by in Roswell, Ga. I graduated from Roswell High School in 2005 and attended Auburn University where I majored in Criminology and Criminal Justice. I received my Bachelor's degree from Auburn University and began my career in Law Enforcement. I was a Police Officer in Montgomery, Al for two years and a Deputy Sheriff at Cherokee County, Ga for 3 1/2 years.

I decided to make a career changee and left my job as a Deputy and am currently attending Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp where I expect to graduate in December 2018.
